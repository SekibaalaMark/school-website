Community Support

At Blessing Junior School Kaswa, we believe in the power of community. Our school thrives because of the dedicated support from parents, local businesses, and community members who volunteer their time, skills, and resources to help our children receive the best education possible.

School Garden Project

Community members work together to maintain our school garden, which provides fresh vegetables for school meals and teaches children about sustainable agriculture.

Parent Volunteers

Our dedicated parents volunteer their time to assist with school events, classroom activities, and administrative tasks to support our teachers and staff.

Building Improvements

Local craftsmen and community members contribute their skills to help maintain and improve our school facilities, creating a better learning environment.

Community Fundraisers

Regular fundraising events bring our community together to raise essential funds for school supplies, scholarships, and special educational programs.

How You Can Get Involved

There are many ways you can contribute to the growth and development of Blessing Junior School Kaswa. Whether you have time, skills, or resources to share, your support makes a significant difference in the lives of our students.

  • Volunteer your time at school events or in classrooms
  • Share your professional skills (carpentry, plumbing, teaching, etc.)
  • Donate educational materials or school supplies
  • Participate in or organize fundraising activities
  • Sponsor a child's education
  • Join our parent-teacher association
