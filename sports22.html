Our Sports Activities

At Blessing Junior School Kaswa, we believe in the importance of sports for physical development, teamwork, and discipline. Our students actively participate in various sports activities throughout the year.

Football

Our football team trains weekly and participates in inter-school tournaments.

Athletics

We encourage running, long jump, and other athletic events during our annual sports day.

Netball

Our netball team fosters teamwork and sportsmanship among our girls.

Located in Kaswa village, Busula Town, Musaale Parish, Katikamu Subcounty, Luwero District.
