Blessing Junior School Girls' Netball Team

Our girls' netball team embodies excellence, teamwork, and determination at Blessing Junior School Kaswa. The team has become a source of pride for our school, demonstrating that with dedication and hard work, our girls can achieve remarkable success both on and off the court. Through netball, our students develop important skills like leadership, communication, and strategic thinking.

Training & Practice

Our netball team trains diligently twice a week, focusing on shooting accuracy, defensive techniques, court positioning, and game strategies to prepare for competitions.

Competition Day

The team regularly participates in district-level tournaments and friendly matches with neighboring schools, showcasing their skills and sportsmanship.
