Our Academic Programs

At Blessing Junior School Kaswa, we provide a comprehensive education that focuses on developing both academic excellence and character. Our curriculum is designed to nurture critical thinking, creativity, and a love for learning.

Nursery Education

Our Nursery education program follows the national curriculum with enhanced learning activities and personalized attention. Our Children in Top class graduate as they advance from Nursery to Primary. This aimed to inspire them to work hard and graduate from University.

Primary Education

We emphasize practical science education and more with hands-on experiments to foster curiosity and scientific thinking. We start from Primary One P.1 up to Primary Seven P.7.
