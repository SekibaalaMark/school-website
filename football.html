Blessing Junior School Football Team

Our boys' football team represents the spirit of teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship at Blessing Junior School Kaswa. The team trains regularly under the guidance of dedicated coaches who focus not only on developing football skills but also on building character, resilience, and leadership qualities in our young players.

Training Sessions

Our team practices three times a week, focusing on ball control, passing techniques, tactical awareness, and physical fitness to prepare for competitions.

Match Day

The team participates in inter-school tournaments and friendly matches throughout the school year, representing our school with pride and determination.

Team Spirit

Beyond the game itself, our football program teaches important life lessons about cooperation, respect for opponents, and how to handle both victory and defeat with grace.

Team Achievements

Our football team has made us proud with several accomplishments in local and district competitions:

2024

Runners-up in Luwero District Primary Schools Tournament

2023

Champions of Busula Town Inter-School Cup
