Music & Performing Arts

At Blessing Junior School Kaswa, we believe that music and performing arts are essential components of a well-rounded education. Our students develop confidence, creativity, and teamwork through singing, dancing, and drama activities. These programs help children express themselves, preserve cultural traditions, and build important life skills.

School Choir

Our talented choir performs traditional and contemporary songs at school events, community gatherings, and music competitions. Students learn vocal techniques, harmony, and the joy of singing together.

Traditional Dance

Students learn and perform traditional Ugandan dances, preserving cultural heritage while developing coordination, rhythm, and an appreciation for their cultural roots.

Drama & Storytelling

Through drama performances, our students develop public speaking skills, creativity, and confidence. They perform plays that entertain while teaching important moral lessons.

More on Drama

Students have the opportunity to act and get a glimpse of how they want to be in Future like "Police men" and more.
